08 Frequently asked questions
How quickly do you respond to new leads?
Speed-to-lead is the foundation of everything we do. New inquiries are contacted within minutes during calling hours — not the next day, and never “when someone gets a chance.” The difference between a five-minute response and a next-day response is usually the difference between a closed sale and a missed opportunity.
Will prospects know they’re speaking with an outsourced sales team?
No. We call as your business, in your brand voice, using scripts built around your services, pricing, and personality. Onboarding exists precisely so that every conversation sounds like it came from inside your four walls.
How do you learn our pricing, programs, and brand voice?
That’s step one of the partnership. Before a single call is made, we sit down with you to document your pricing, programs, promotions, objection handling, and the way your business talks to its customers. Your scripts are built from that — nothing generic, nothing recycled.
What CRM systems do you work with?
We work inside your existing CRM rather than forcing you onto ours — including the platforms most common in your industry. If your current setup has gaps, we’ll recommend and configure what’s needed during onboarding so every call, note, and outcome is logged where you can see it.
Do you work with single locations or multi-location operators?
Both. Single-location owners get a full sales team without adding headcount. Multi-location operators get consistent sales execution and identical reporting across every location — which is exactly where in-house follow-up tends to fall apart first.
How do you report results?
Weekly. Every Monday you receive a MACRO report covering calls made, conversations held, appointments booked, sales closed, and our recommendations for the week ahead. You’ll never have to ask what happened with your leads — the answer is already in your inbox.
What industries do you specialize in?
Our deepest specialization is boutique fitness franchises — studios, group training, and membership-based brands — where our track record was built. The same system now serves service businesses of every kind that grow by phone. If your customers call, book, or inquire, we can work your pipeline.
How quickly can we get started?
Onboarding — learning your business and building your sales system — typically takes a matter of days, not months. Most partners have Aphani actively working their pipeline within the first two weeks of signing on.
Is there a contract?
We keep terms simple and transparent, and we’ll walk through them line by line on your strategy call. Our goal is a partnership that keeps earning its place every month — the weekly reporting exists so you can hold us to that.
