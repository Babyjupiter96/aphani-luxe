01 Case studies
$17,006 in month one
Minnesota location · Boutique group fitness

The challenge

A well-run studio with strong classes and a loyal community — and a CRM holding months of leads, missed trials, and former members that nobody on staff had time to call. Marketing kept generating interest; the follow-up couldn’t keep up.

What Aphani implemented

Full outbound coverage from week one: same-day response on every new lead, a multi-touch cadence across the aging database, and a structured win-back campaign for former members — all scripted in the studio’s own voice, with results reported weekly.

The outcome

$17,006 in new membership revenue in the first month of the partnership — from opportunities that were already sitting in the studio’s database.

5.49× ROI in 90 days
Iowa location · Boutique group fitness

The challenge

Paid advertising was producing leads, but follow-up stopped after one or two attempts. The cost per lead was fine — the cost per membership wasn’t, because most leads never got a real sales conversation.

What Aphani implemented

Aphani took ownership of every lead the day it arrived and ran a disciplined 8–12 touch cadence — over 1,400 calls a month against the pipeline — with weekly MACRO reporting so the owner could watch ad spend turn into revenue.

The outcome

A 5.49× return on investment within the first ninety days. The same marketing budget, finally followed through to the close.

87.5% trial close rate
Iowa location · Best in the brand’s network

The challenge

Trials were booking steadily, but too many finished their trial period and walked away without ever hearing a confident membership conversation. Conversion depended on whoever happened to be at the front desk.

What Aphani implemented

A first-call close discipline: needs assessment, value build, close — with the trial positioned as the fallback rather than the opener. Every trial member got a structured conversation at exactly the right moment.

The outcome

87.5% of trials converted to membership — the best rate in the brand’s national network for the period.
